About Me


I am a Master's student in Physics at Savitribai Phule Pune University. I have a keen interest in experimental high energy physics, particularly in the field of particle physics. I am passionate about using data science and developing software tools for analysis in HEP. My works primarily focus on the study of fundamental particles using experimental data from the CMS detector at the LHC at CERN. I have developed a pipeline to streamline the analysis of CMS Open Data allowing efficient processing of large and complex datasets.
